## Brand Core From PDF

Yen May Vintage is built around the idea that aesthetic taste becomes a personal statement. The brand is not just selling secondhand clothes. It is selling a curated point of view: a quiet, slow, floating feeling where people pause, look closely, and choose something that feels like it belongs to them.

Core concept:

- Yen May is a place for finding identity through clothes.
- Each item is selected through layers of feeling and aesthetic judgment, not just because it looks good.
- Fashion is not about owning more; it is about finding the right thing that fits who the customer is becoming.
- The brand should feel light, calm, cinematic, emotional, and personal.

Core spirit:

- Organic and aged.
- Shopping becomes a hunt for identity.
- Clothing becomes a tool for self-love and personal potential.

## Facebook Fanpage Rules

Facebook is new, so use the early page boost period carefully.

Facebook serves two buyer funnels at the same time:

- Vietnamese customers: discover products on Facebook, then inbox to ask price, reserve items, and buy through DM.
- Foreign travelers: discover Yen May through Facebook content, verify address/hours/vibe, then visit the physical shop.

Facebook copy and CTAs should split by content intent:

- Product posts for Vietnamese buyers: use `DM de giu hang`, price, size, condition, and urgency.
- Discovery/location/travel posts for foreign visitors: use `Open daily 10:00-21:00`, address, Google Maps/location cue, and `Get Directions` CTA.

Instagram funnel:

- Foreign customers often prefer buying through Instagram DM.
- IG should use English-first captions and Stories for foreign-facing content, with Vietnamese support where useful. Skip Russian for now.
- IG product Stories/Highlights should make it easy to ask availability, size, price, and reserve via DM.

Rules:

- Upload native video, never TikTok watermark links.
- Use clear 3-4 line captions.
- End with a concrete CTA such as "DM de hoi gia".
- Post 4-5 times per week.
- Reuse TikTok videos as Facebook Reels only after removing watermark.
- Boost only posts that reach more than 200 organic reach within the first 6 hours.
- Paid targeting: Da Nang, Hoi An, 30 km radius, interests in vintage clothing, Y2K fashion, thrift shopping, sustainable fashion.

## Instagram Rules

- Instagram is a major buying channel for foreign customers, not just a brand channel.
- Foreign customers like buying through Instagram, so IG content should support DM-based buying as well as shop visits.

## Critique And Risk Controls From Evaluation Document

The evaluation document scores the marketing plan at 74/100. It agrees that the differentiation, hook psychology, TikTok/Instagram strategy, and series concepts are strong. Its main concern is execution risk: agency dependency, thin Facebook launch mechanics, manual tracking burden, weak conversion triggers, and no clear retention loop.

Use this critique as launch hardening, not as a replacement for the PDF or marketing plan.

Key gaps to fix:

- Add urgency for Vietnamese local buyers, especially because average product price is around 250k VND and sits in impulse-buy territory.
- Separate pricing psychology by tier: 150k-250k impulse, 300k-500k considered, 1M+ premium/branded.
- Separate CTA by audience: Vietnamese buyers should get "DM de giu hang"; foreign travelers should get location, hours, and Google Maps direction.
- Add retention loop: second-time buyers get Close Friends / early drop access.
- Fix Facebook new page launch with day-1 friend invites, group sharing, and a small first-post boost instead of waiting for organic reach.
- Track TikTok manually before 1,000 followers: 24h views, 48h views, comments, shares, saves.
- Turn the first shoot into a specific agency brief, because the agency has not worked in niche vintage before.

Agency brief requirements:

- Tone reference should feel closer to a pottery studio, artisan workshop, or specialty coffee space than a generic fashion shop.
- Do not use generic upbeat shop music.
- Do not open with logo/name zooms.
- Do not default to fast-cut haul style.
- Every first-wave video needs a hook in the first 1.5 seconds.
- Every Y2K/vintage video needs text overlay with "Y2K Da Nang" or "vintage shop Da Nang".
- Use ASMR fabric/rack sound for POV alley, Reject Pile, and Morning ASMR videos; use trend-aware Y2K sound only where it fits.

First 5 videos, in priority order:

1. POV alley into shop reveal.
2. Reject Pile number 1.
3. The 1 in 300 hero product.
4. Find Your Era number 1.
5. Shop morning ASMR.

Immediate launch fixes:

- Create and share the agency brief before the first shoot.
- Invite Facebook friends on day 1 and boost the first post with 200k-300k VND if needed.
- Add urgency signals to product captions and Stories, such as "thuong di trong 24h" and sold overlays.
