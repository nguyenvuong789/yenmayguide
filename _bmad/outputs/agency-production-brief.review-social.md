## Original Findings And Resolutions

The findings below are kept as an audit trail. They have already been patched into `_bmad/outputs/agency-production-brief.md`; see the resolution summary above for where each fix landed.

### P1 - Missing final deliverables list

The brief says what to shoot, but not exactly what the agency must deliver after the shoot.

Patch recommendation:

Add a `Final Deliverables` section:

- 5 edited vertical videos, 9:16.
- 5 clean no-watermark exports.
- 5 caption drafts.
- 5 thumbnail/cover frame options.
- 20-30 raw clips.
- 20 Google Maps-ready photos.
- 10 Instagram Story product assets.
- Folder naming convention.

### P1 - Missing approval workflow

The brief has a posting checklist, but not who approves and when.

Patch recommendation:

Add:

- Agency sends first cut before posting.
- Shop/owner approves product info, condition, price, and curation reason.
- No product video goes live until availability is confirmed.
- No boosted post without shop approval.

### P1 - No language versioning rule per asset

The brief says English/Vietnamese in general, but the agency may not know whether each video needs EN, VI, or both.

Patch recommendation:

Add language rules:

- V1: English-first, small Vietnamese support optional.
- V2: Vietnamese-first or bilingual, because it explains selection.
- V3: match buyer target; English if IG/TikTok foreign-facing, Vietnamese if FB product-facing.
- V4: English-first for save/share.
- V5: English-first.

### P2 - Text overlay volume may be too high for V1

V1 has 5 overlay steps in a 15-20 second video. That can work, but it may clutter the visual if the agency uses big text.

Patch recommendation:

Limit overlay to 3-4 beats:

1. `An Thuong area`
2. `Follow the alley`
3. `Curated Y2K & vintage in Da Nang`
4. `Open daily 10:00-21:00`

CTA can live in caption or end frame.

### P2 - V2 Reject Pile could become negative if not handled carefully

Showing rejected items is powerful, but if it feels like mocking bad products, it can look arrogant.

Patch recommendation:

Add rule:

Do not ridicule rejected pieces. Show neutral reasons: fabric, form, condition, or brand fit. Tone should be educational, not superior.

### P2 - Cross-posting needs platform-specific edits

The brief says cross-post, but not what changes by platform.

Patch recommendation:

Add:

- TikTok: strongest hook and search keyword.
- Instagram: cleaner cover frame and more refined caption.
- Facebook: native upload, shorter Vietnamese caption, clear DM/Get Directions CTA.
- Google Maps: photo-only, no overly editorial filters.

### P2 - Threads is under-specified

"1 supporting note per main video" is fine, but the agency may not know whether they manage Threads or the shop does.

Patch recommendation:

Assign owner:

- Agency drafts Threads notes.
- Shop approves/posts, or agency posts if given access.

### P3 - Missing raw audio capture requirements

ASMR is central to the concept. The brief should specify audio capture quality.

Patch recommendation:

Add:

- Capture 10-20 seconds of clean room tone.
- Record fabric/rack/tag sounds separately when possible.
- Avoid loud street noise for ASMR clips.
