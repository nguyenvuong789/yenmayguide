## Original Findings And Resolutions

The findings below are kept as an audit trail. They have already been patched into `_bmad/outputs/agency-production-brief.md`; see the resolution summary above for where each fix landed.

### P1 - Need final caption drafts for V1-V5

The agency has overlay text, but not complete captions. They may write generic captions.

Patch recommendation:

Add ready-to-use caption drafts for each video.

Example:

V1 caption:

`A hidden Y2K & vintage shop in An Hai Dong 1, Da Nang. Curated pieces, quiet alley, open daily 10:00-21:00. Get Directions.`

V2 caption:

`Yên Mây không tuyển đồ theo số lượng. Mỗi món phải qua nhiều lớp chọn: nhãn mác rõ, chất vải tốt, form đẹp, tình trạng chỉn chu, màu sắc có gu và một điểm riêng đủ đáng giữ lại. DM để hỏi những món đã qua vòng chọn.`

### P1 - Some hooks are still too soft

`This was the only one I kept` is good. `One piece made it through today` is okay but less vivid. V4 hooks are mostly educational but not scroll-stopping enough.

Patch recommendation:

Add stronger hook bank:

- `I rejected the pile before choosing this one.`
- `This detail is why it stayed.`
- `Most thrift shops would keep this. Yen May did not.`
- `Before you buy Y2K in Da Nang, check this detail.`
- `The alley is easy to miss. The rack is not.`

### P1 - CTA wording needs hierarchy

The brief lists several CTAs under "Must Do In Every Video". Agencies may think all CTAs are available everywhere.

Patch recommendation:

Add:

- Use only one CTA per video.
- Discovery videos: `Get Directions`.
- Product videos: `DM to check size and availability` or `DM để giữ hàng`.
- Education videos: `Save this before your next vintage hunt`.
- Story/product cards: `DM to hold for 24h`.

### P2 - Text overlay for V2 mixes English and Vietnamese abruptly

V2 overlay starts Vietnamese, then English reject reasons, then English owner line. This may feel inconsistent.

Patch recommendation:

Choose one primary language per version.

V2 Vietnamese overlay:

- `Yên Mây không tuyển đồ theo số lượng`
- `Loại: vải quá mỏng`
- `Loại: form không giữ dáng`
- `Giữ lại: màu + form + tình trạng`

V2 English overlay:

- `Yen May does not buy by volume`
- `Passed on: fabric too thin`
- `Passed on: weak shape`
- `Kept: color + shape + condition`

### P2 - V5 has atmosphere but weak emotional line

Morning ASMR can become pretty but forgettable. It needs a memorable line.

Patch recommendation:

Add one of:

- `Before the rack opens, every piece gets one more look.`
- `A quiet morning before the first find.`
- `The shop opens at 10. The choosing starts earlier.`

### P2 - "owner" in public Vietnamese copy may feel slightly unnatural

The owner does not want the public name shown, but "owner" inside Vietnamese copy can feel half-English.

Patch recommendation:

Use depending on context:

- Public poetic copy: `chủ shop` when the human selector needs to be explicit, or `Yên Mây` when the sentence can stay brand-led.
- Internal/brief: `owner`
- Product caption: `Yên Mây giữ lại món này vì...`

Suggested replacement for public copy:

`Mỗi món phải qua mắt chọn rất kỹ của chủ shop trước.`

But keep `owner` internally if that is preferred.

### P3 - Need anti-examples

The brief says "not generic", but agency may still not understand.

Patch recommendation:

Add examples:

Do not write:

- `Hàng mới về siêu xinh, ghé shop nha`
- `Vintage cực chất tại Đà Nẵng`
- `Nhanh tay inbox ngay`

Use instead:

- `Giữ lại vì form vai và màu này không dễ gặp lại.`
- `Picked for the label, fabric, and the way it falls on the body.`
- `Open daily 10:00-21:00. Get Directions.`
