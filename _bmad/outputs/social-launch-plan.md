## Launch Objective

Launch Yen May Vintage across 5 channels with a clear split between:

- discovery content that makes the shop worth finding
- curation proof that makes the brand credible
- product content that creates DM inquiries and reservations
- Google Maps content that converts nearby visitors and travelers

Phase 1 keyword focus:

- `Y2K Da Nang`
- `Y2K shop Da Nang`
- `vintage shop Da Nang`
- `curated vintage Da Nang`
- `An Hai Dong 1`

## Channel Roles

### Facebook

Role:

- Vietnamese DM buying.
- Foreign traveler validation.
- Local credibility.

Primary CTA:

- Page-level: `Get Directions`
- Product-level: `DM để giữ hàng`

Language:

- Vietnamese-first for product posts.
- English support for discovery/location posts when useful.

### Instagram

Role:

- Main visual catalog.
- Foreign customer DM buying.
- Stories/Highlights storefront.

Primary CTA:

- `DM to check size and availability`
- `DM to hold for 24h`

Language:

- English-first for foreign-facing content.
- Vietnamese where local buying intent is clear.
- No Russian.

### TikTok

Role:

- Search/discovery engine.
- Hook-led video growth.
- Curation proof.

Primary CTA:

- `Save this for your Da Nang trip`
- `DM to check size and availability`
- `Get Directions`

Language:

- English-first or bilingual depending on video.
- Keyword must appear in overlay/caption when relevant.

### Threads

Role:

- Low-effort daily presence.
- Shop diary.
- Drop notes.
- Support main content.

Primary CTA:

- Soft CTA only.

### Google Maps

Role:

- Local search and direction conversion.
- Traveler trust.
- Review engine.

Primary CTA:

- `Get Directions`

## Week 4: Measure And Scale

Goal:

- Review early data and scale the best-performing formats.

Tasks:

- Identify top 2 videos by:
  - 24h views
  - 48h views
  - saves
  - shares
  - DMs
  - direction clicks/comments asking location
- Recut top video into another platform version.
- Produce one follow-up based on comments/questions.
- Decide whether to boost any Facebook/Instagram post.

Boost rule:

- Do not boost weak posts.
- Consider boosting only if:
  - organic reach is promising
  - CTA is clear
  - post has strong save/share/comment/DM signal
  - product availability or visit CTA is still valid

Review meeting agenda:

1. Which hook worked?
2. Which CTA drove action?
3. Which platform created DMs?
4. Which content created direction/visit intent?
5. Which products sold or got holds?
6. What should the agency shoot next?

## Weekly Minimum Cadence

Minimum viable cadence:

- TikTok: 3 videos/week.
- Instagram: daily Stories, 3 Reels/week, 2 Feed posts/week.
- Facebook: 4 posts/week, including 2 native Reels.
- Threads: 3 posts/week.
- Google Maps: 3 photo updates/week.

If capacity is limited:

1. Prioritize TikTok + Instagram Reels.
2. Keep Instagram Stories active.
3. Native repost best Reels to Facebook.
4. Upload Google Maps photos.
5. Use Threads as low-effort support only.

## Measurement During Launch

Track daily for the first 14 days:

- TikTok 24h and 48h views.
- TikTok comments, shares, saves.
- Instagram DMs asking size/availability.
- Instagram Story replies.
- Holds created.
- Holds paid within 24h.
- Facebook DMs.
- Facebook direction-related comments.
- Google Maps views/direction clicks if available.
- Store visits mentioning TikTok/Instagram/Facebook/Google.

Track weekly:

- Search manually for `Y2K Da Nang`.
- Search manually for `Y2K shop Da Nang`.
- Review Google Maps photos and Q&A completeness.
- Check which content generated real buyer behavior, not just views.

## Launch Guardrails

- Do not publish product content without confirmed availability.
- Do not use owner name or face as public-facing content.
- Do not claim exact era/year without evidence.
- Do not invent origin stories.
- Do not upload TikTok-watermarked videos to Facebook or Instagram.
- Do not use urgency unless true.
- Do not let Google Maps photos look blurry, dark, over-filtered, or confusing.
