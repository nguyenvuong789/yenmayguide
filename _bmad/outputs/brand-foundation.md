## Brand Idea

Yen May Vintage is where aesthetic taste becomes a personal statement.

Plain-language version:

`Curated Y2K and vintage pieces for people who want to dress like themselves, not everyone else.`

The PDF's core idea:

`YÊN MÂY VINTAGE: KHI "GU" THẨM MỸ TRỞ THÀNH BẢN TUYÊN NGÔN ĐỘC BẢN`

The brand is not about selling a large pile of secondhand clothes. It is about a selected point of view: quiet, slow, tactile, and personal. People come to Yên Mây to find an item that feels like it belongs to who they are becoming.

Yên Mây is named as a feeling: light, slow, and slightly suspended like a cloud. It should feel like a place quiet enough for someone to pause, look closely, and choose something that genuinely belongs to them.

Core belief:

- Fashion is not about having more.
- Fashion is about finding the right piece.
- The selector's eye is part of the product.
- Shopping becomes a search for identity, not just a transaction.
- Clothing can help someone love themselves and unlock a more personal version of who they are.

## Curation Criteria

Yen May's curation should be presented as the owner's strict selection standard, while keeping the owner personally low-profile. The brand should not say "curated" as a vague claim; it should show the criteria behind each piece.

Core curation promise:

`Yen May does not buy by volume. Every piece has to pass several filters: clear label, good fabric, strong shape, polished condition, tasteful color, and one detail worth keeping.`

Vietnamese version:

`Yên Mây không tuyển đồ theo số lượng. Mỗi món phải qua nhiều lớp chọn: nhãn mác rõ, chất vải tốt, form đẹp, tình trạng chỉn chu, màu sắc có gu và một điểm riêng đủ đáng giữ lại.`

### Selection Criteria

1. Clear Brand Or Origin Signal

- Prioritize pieces with a clear, trustworthy brand, label, or product signal.
- Branded pieces and reputable labels get priority.
- Avoid unclear mass-market pieces that feel like generic China/Guangzhou stock.
- Do not overclaim authenticity unless proof is visible.

Copy angle:

- `Picked because the label, fabric, and construction all felt right.`
- `Ưu tiên những món có thương hiệu/nhãn mác rõ, chất liệu và form đáng giữ.`

2. Not China/Guangzhou Fast-Fashion Feel

- Yen May should not feel like a shop reselling generic China/Guangzhou stock.
- Avoid pieces that look overly mass-produced, trend-copy, thin, or disposable.
- If a piece is selected without a known label, it must pass harder on fabric, form, condition, and uniqueness.

Copy angle:

- `This made the cut because it does not feel like disposable trendwear.`
- `Không chọn kiểu hàng đại trà mặc vài lần là chán.`

3. Condition Standard

- No heavy flaws.
- No serious stains, tears, deformation, broken closures, major pilling, or fabric damage that weakens the piece.
- Minor flaws are acceptable only when they do not affect wearability or overall value.
- If a minor flaw exists, disclose it clearly in product copy.

Copy angle:

- `Minor flaw noted, still wearable and worth keeping.`
- `Có lỗi nhẹ đã ghi rõ; không nhận lỗi nặng làm mất giá trị món đồ.`

4. Newness And Wearability

- Prioritize pieces that still feel fresh, clean, wearable, and ready for real use.
- The item should not feel tired, old in a bad way, or kept only because it is secondhand.
- "Vintage" should feel selected, not worn out.

Copy angle:

- `Vintage, but still fresh enough to live in now.`
- `Đồ cũ nhưng không cũ kỹ - vẫn đủ mới để mặc đẹp hôm nay.`

5. Unique Detail

- Each piece should have at least one reason to remember it.
- This can be color, cut, fabric, texture, label, silhouette, print, hardware, or styling potential.
- Avoid pieces that are technically fine but forgettable.

Copy angle:

- `Kept for the color first, then the cut.`
- `Giữ lại vì màu sắc và chi tiết này không dễ gặp lại.`

6. Color And Visual Freshness

- Prioritize colors that photograph well and fit Yen May's warm, soft, cinematic identity.
- Color should feel fresh, intentional, and wearable in Da Nang.
- Avoid colors that feel harsh, overly saturated, or disconnected from the brand unless the piece is intentionally Y2K and strong enough to carry it.

Copy angle:

- `The color is the reason this piece stopped the sorting process.`
- `Màu này lên hình đẹp và vẫn dễ mặc ở Đà Nẵng.`

7. Fit And Styling Potential

- A piece should have styling life beyond one photo.
- Prioritize items that can become a strong outfit: soft girl, vintage muse, beach runway, art girl, Y2K street, or collector piece.
- If the item is harder to style, show exactly how to wear it.

Copy angle:

- `Easy to style, but not easy to replace.`
- `Dễ phối nhưng vẫn có điểm riêng.`

### Reject Criteria

Reject pieces that are:

- Generic China/Guangzhou stock feel.
- Thin, disposable, or overly trend-copy.
- Heavy stain, tear, deformation, broken zipper/button, or fabric damage.
- Too tired to feel valuable.
- Forgettable even if technically wearable.
- Off-brand color or texture.
- Unclear quality with no compensating uniqueness.

### Instagram Buying Flow

Goal:

- Reduce Activation Energy for foreign customers and Vietnamese buyers who prefer Instagram DM.
- Make buying feel simple, clear, and low-risk.

Flow:

1. Customer sees an item on Story, Reel, Carousel, or Highlight.
2. Customer DMs to ask size, availability, price, or more details.
3. Shop replies with:
   - item availability
   - size
   - price
   - condition
   - curation reason
   - more photos/video if needed
4. If customer confirms, shop can hold the item for `24h`.
5. Customer chooses:
   - pickup at shop
   - shipping
6. Payment method:
   - Vietnamese bank transfer
7. After payment or final confirmation, update Story/Highlight with `sold` where useful.

Default hold rule:

- Hold time: `24h`.
- If no payment/confirmation after 24h, the item can return to available status.

DM copy template:

English:

`Yes, this piece is available. Size: [x]. Price: [x]. Condition: [x]. We can hold it for 24h. Pickup at the shop or shipping is available. Payment by Vietnamese bank transfer.`

Vietnamese:

`Món này còn nha. Size: [x]. Giá: [x]. Tình trạng: [x]. Yên Mây có thể giữ hàng 24h. Bạn có thể ghé shop lấy hoặc hỏi ship. Thanh toán bằng chuyển khoản ngân hàng Việt Nam.`

Psychology:

- Activation Energy: DM is the first easy step.
- Regret Aversion: size, condition, price, and more photos reduce uncertainty.
- Commitment: 24h hold creates a clear next step without fake pressure.
- Hick's Law: only two fulfillment choices - pickup or shipping.

## Price And Conversion Framing

Use tier-specific framing:

- `150k-250k`: impulse tier. Make price, size, condition, and DM action immediate. Use urgency only when true.
- `300k-500k`: considered tier. Explain value, fabric, durability, and styling.
- `1M+`: premium/branded tier. Use label proof, condition close-ups, styling value, scarcity when true, and retail/reference anchoring only when accurate.

Do not over-explain cheap/impulse products. Make them easy to buy.
